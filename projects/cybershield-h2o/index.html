CyberShield H₂O

Years: 2024-Present

CyberShield H₂O is an interactive, educational game designed to raise awareness about cybersecurity threats to our nation's drinking water systems, which are often under-resourced and vulnerable to attack. Through scenario-based learning, the game trains students to think critically about water system risks, anticipate and respond to cyber incidents, and understand the public health and economic importance of this infrastructure. The project promotes cybersecurity careers within municipal water operations and incorporates STEM and cybersecurity concepts.
