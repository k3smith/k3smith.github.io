Operationally Directed Instructional Network (ODIN)

Role: Principal Investigator

Years: 2020 - Present

ODIN is the data management system for the Navy's Afloat Maintenance Training Strategy (NAMTS). It provides the Commander Navy Regional Maintenance Center (CNRMC) and over 230 users with secure, real-time access to training and maintenance data for more than 2,600 Sailors worldwide, both ashore and afloat. Through comprehensive reporting, live dashboards, and API integration, ODIN-EL delivers the actionable intelligence needed to effectively manage personnel and enhance naval operational readiness.
