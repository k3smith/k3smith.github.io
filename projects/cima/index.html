CIMA Recovery

Years: 2021 - Present

When disaster strikes, recovery can be slow and unequal. CIMA is a powerful platform to change that. Built in partnership with community leaders, CIMA connects vital resources – volunteers and materials – with families needing urgent home repairs. We're building smart scheduling algorithms and user-friendly interfaces to ensure aid is fast, efficient, and prioritizes those most vulnerable. CIMA is our commitment to using technology to speed up recovery, reduce hardship, and build stronger, more resilient communities.
